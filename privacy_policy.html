Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Bookmarked – PDF Page Tracker is a Chrome extension that helps you save your reading page and notes across devices. Your privacy is important to us.

Data we collect

We collect only the data you explicitly enter into the extension:

  • Book or document titles
  • Page numbers
  • Optional notes

How your data is stored

All data is stored exclusively using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API. This means:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device and synced via your Google account to other devices where you are signed in.
  • We do not operate any servers, databases, or backend infrastructure.
  • We never transmit your data to any third-party service or external server.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or otherwise disclose your data to any third parties. Ever.

Permissions

This extension requests only the storage permission, which is required to save and sync your bookmarks. No other browser data, tabs, history, or personal information is accessed.

Deleting your data

You can remove all your bookmarks at any time using the "Remove" button within the extension, or by uninstalling the extension entirely, which clears all locally stored data.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date above. Given the simple, local-only nature of this extension, changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub page.
